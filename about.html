About

I'm a life time learner. I come from a family on nine chilren. My dad used to call it the only family of nine only children since we we space so far apart with a 28 year age span from youngest to oldest. I have lived from Pennsylvania to California. I like to Say I have done everything in Life. I have been a soldier, farm worker, factory worker, Commercial RealEstate Appraiser, pharmacy technician, Uber driver, Coder, in finance at an automotive plant, landlord, delivery driver, manager at a car wash, manager at a Casino party company, and a mix of other things. My current passion is mysticism and shaminism. I enjoy meditating and journeying within the mind and getting intouch with the subconcious. I find it is important to stop and listin to your intution and feelings to keep a balanced life. My personal phylosophy is to balance doing good in the world with enjoying it personally. If I find I haven't done enough for others I make a point of doing so and if I find I haven;t been paying attentin to me I make a point to take time for myself and try to keep them balanced.
