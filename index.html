The Battle

I dreamt a dream of mighty battles arrayed Though deeper truths it portrayed My Father, My Mother, my Sisters, my Brothers These I recognized and no others Each a warrior of light Each an arch angel with the power of flight One by one they fell, but for them there is no hell. They fought the fight, They pushed back the night. Each would sit and talk awhile Lift an eye and raise a smile Not a one of them could be reviled. At this realization I just smiled. Each of them, a gift did they impart Without their gifts I could not start.

The Battle

I dreamt a dream of mighty battles arrayed
Though deeper truths it portrayed
My Father, My Mother, my Sisters, my Brothers
These I recognized and no others
Each a warrior of light
Each an arch angel with the power of flight
One by one they fell
but for them there is no hell
They fought the fight
They pushed back the night
Each would sit and talk awhile
Lift an eye and raise a smile
Not a one of them could be reviled
At this realization I just smiled
Each of them, a gift did they impart
Without their gifts I could not start.
